UNLOCKING AUTHENTIC INNOVATION

True innovation isn't just jargon; it's a balanced equation of three core elements.

In essence, innovation is the sweet spot where desirability, viability, and feasibility collide. Overused? Absolutely. But let's not kill the messenger; let's redefine the message.

Check out my simple infographic that decodes the DNA of real innovation. Looks simple, but it's far from a walk in the park.


Desirability : Is there a genuine demand for it?

Viability : Will it be profitable?

Feasibility : Can we actually pull this off?


Keep these trio of factors on your radar, from the drawing board to the big reveal, for any product or service you're cooking up.

Missing Desirability ? You risk missing your target audience. Where's the appeal?

Lacking Viability ? You'll need a solid business case. What's the financial upside?

And without Feasibility , you're aiming too high. Have you considered the technical constraints?
